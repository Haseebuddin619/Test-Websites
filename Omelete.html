Crispy Fried Omelete

Omelete is basically a fried egg. But there are many types of recipies related to this. Today we will make a really crispy fired egg that doesnt smell and tastes great and crispy. Egg is one of the cheapest and most nutritious foods one can eat, so thats why im making it today

Ingredients

  • An Egg
  • Salt, Pepper, Basic Seasonings
  • Spinach or some Leaves
  • Cooking Oil and Butter

Instructions

  1. First take 3 tablespoons of oil and a little piece of butter and let it heat up in the pan.
  2. Once it gets to the appropriate heat, crack an egg and put it into the pan.
  3. You can either stir (scrambled) or let it form a shape on its own.
  4. Add appropriate amounts of salt, pepper or other seasonsing as your preferences.
  5. Add spinach or other leaves to you liking and let it cook, but dont overcook ti.
  6. Once the egg gets golden brown, stop cooking and enjoy you meal :)
